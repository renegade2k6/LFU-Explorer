War Room - Spiritual Link System

System Overview

The War Room is a building that unlocks the Spiritual Link system - a revolutionary mechanic that allows players to automatically level up heroes without spending traditional upgrade resources.

Key Features:

Automatic Hero Leveling Non-leader heroes placed in Link Slots instantly reach the Link Level

Leader Hero System Top 5 highest-level heroes determine the base Link Level

Resource-Free Leveling Heroes upgraded through the Link don't consume traditional resources

Refundable Upgrades When heroes are placed in slots, previous upgrade costs are refunded

Hero Bonds System 37 Hero Bonds provide additional attributes and Link Level cap increases

Hero Statues 78 hero statues can be activated when heroes reach Level 80 through the Link

Quality Restrictions Hero initial quality determines maximum Link Level (Common heroes cannot use the system)

Slot Expansion Up to 40 Link Slots can be unlocked using Link Chips

Spiritual Link Mechanics

Leader Heroes

Definition: The top 5 heroes with the highest hero level are automatically identified as "Leader Heroes" and placed into designated slots of the Spiritual Link.

How It Works: 1. The system automatically identifies your 5 highest-level heroes 2. These heroes are placed into special Leader Hero slots 3. The lowest level among the five leader heroes becomes the Link Level 4. Example: If your 5 leader heroes are levels 120, 115, 110, 105, and 100, the Link Level is 100

Important Notes: - Leader heroes determine the Link Level but are not subject to the Link themselves - All other heroes placed in Link Slots will instantly reach the current Link Level -

Link Level System

Link Level is the automatic level that non-leader heroes reach when placed in Link Slots.

Key Rules:

  1. Base Link Level: Determined by the lowest level of the 5 leader heroes

  2. Advanced Link Level: Once all 5 leader heroes reach Level 100, players can spend Link EXP and Link Coins to increase the Link Level beyond 100

  3. Combat Level Cap: The Link Level cannot exceed the Combat Level (determined by War Room building level)

  4. War Room must be in Advanced Mode to increase Link Level beyond 100

  5. Hero Quality Restrictions:

  6. Common quality heroes: Cannot be leveled through Spiritual Link
  7. Uncommon or Rare quality heroes: Can be upgraded to up to Level 100

  8. Elite or above quality heroes: Can be upgraded to more than Level 100

  9. Myth Quality Bonus: For every Myth quality hero you have, the Link Level cap increases by 1 level

  10. Hero Bond Bonus: Activating Hero Bonds can also raise the Link Level cap

Link Slots

Total Slots Available: 40 slots (IDs 1-40)

Slot Unlock Costs:

Each slot requires Link Chips to unlock:

Slot Range Unlock Cost (Per Slot)
Slots 1-4 30, 50, 70, 100 Link Chips
Slots 5-40 100 Link Chips each

Slot Mechanics:

  1. Instant Level Up: When a hero is placed in a Link Slot, they instantly reach the current Link Level (no upgrade cost)

  2. Resource Refund: If a hero already upgraded to a certain level is placed into a slot, the hero's upgrade cost is returned to you

  3. Slot Cooldown: When you remove a hero from a slot, the hero resets to Level 1 and the slot enters a cooldown period

  5. Simultaneous Level Up: When the Link Level is increased, all heroes' levels in the slots increase at the same time

  6. Statue Activation: Heroes reaching Level 80 through the Spiritual Link can also activate their statues

Link Level Cap

The maximum Link Level is determined by multiple factors:

  1. War Room Combat Level: Base cap determined by building level
  2. Myth Quality Heroes: +1 level per Myth quality hero owned
  3. Hero Bonds: Activating Hero Bonds increases the cap further

Example Calculation: - War Room Level 20: Combat Level 140 - 3 Myth Quality Heroes: +3 levels - Hero Bonds activated: Variable bonus - Total Potential Link Level Cap: 143+ (depending on bonds)

Hero Bonds

Total Bonds: 37 Hero Bonds (IDs 1-40, with some IDs unused)

Hero Bonds provide attribute bonuses and Link Level increases when specific hero combinations reach certain quality tiers.

Bond Quality Requirements

Each bond has 3 quality tier requirements:

Quality Tier Attribute Bonus Link Level Bonus
Tier 1 (Quality 4) Minor stat boost None
Tier 2 (Quality 7) Moderate stat boost None
Tier 3 (Quality 11) Major stat boost +1 to Link Level cap

Bond Activation Benefits: - Tier 1: Provides minor attribute bonuses to all heroes in the bond - Tier 2: Provides moderate attribute bonuses to all heroes in the bond - Tier 3: Provides major attribute bonuses AND increases your maximum Link Level cap by 1

Currencies & Resources

Link EXP

Description: Used to level up the Spiritual Link

Purpose: Required to increase the Link Level beyond 100 after all 5 leader heroes reach Level 100.

Link Coin

Description: Used to level up the Spiritual Link

Purpose: Required alongside Link EXP to increase the Link Level beyond 100.

Link Chip

Description: Used to unlock more slots for the Spiritual Link

Purpose: Required to unlock additional Link Slots (slots 1-40).

Slot Unlock Costs: - Slot 1: 30 Link Chips - Slot 2: 50 Link Chips - Slot 3: 70 Link Chips - Slots 4-40: 100 Link Chips each

Total to Unlock All 40 Slots: 30 + 50 + 70 + (37 100) = 3,850 Link Chips
