APC System

About APCs

APCs (Armored Personnel Carriers) are powerful combat vehicles that complement your hero lineup. Unlike heroes, APCs are specialized units with unique progression systems, customizable parts, and distinct combat roles.

Key Features:

  • Independent Progression: Each APC has its own leveling system separate from heroes
  • Customizable Parts: Upgrade APCs with refitting parts for enhanced abilities
  • Active Skills: Many APCs feature powerful active and passive abilities
  • Cosmetic Skins: Personalize your vehicles with exclusive skins (Hero Rally)
  • Quality Tiers: Vehicles range from Common to Legendary rarity

APC Progression Guide

Early Game (Player Level 1-30)

  • Start: Wasteland APC (automatically available)
  • First Upgrade: Plunderer as soon as possible
  • Focus on leveling Plunderer to max (20)
  • Learn refitting system with Plunderer's upgrade slots
  • Save resources for mid-game Epic vehicles

Mid Game (Player Level 30-50)

  • Acquire Epic tier: Armed Chariot (offense) or Iron Fist (defense)
  • Invest in refitting parts and gadgets
  • Start working toward New World Protector for higher level cap
  • Balance APC upgrades with hero progression
  • Don't overinvest - save for Legendary tier

Late Game (Player Level 50+)

  • Goal: Acquire at least one Legendary APC
  • Options:
    • Demon - Maximum damage for serious spenders
    • Neon - Balanced Legendary for F2P players
    • Pink Note - Long-term investment for active players
  • Max out supreme levels (50)
  • Optimize gadget quality to Ultimate tier
  • Collect Hero Rally skins if available

Resource Management Tips

  • Don't upgrade everything: Focus on 1-2 APCs per tier
  • Prioritize main APC: Get one vehicle to max before spreading resources
  • Gadget quality matters: Ultimate-tier gadgets provide massive stat boosts
  • Event participation: Many exclusive APCs and skins come from events
  • Maintenance costs: Higher-tier APCs cost more to repair - use strategically

  • Expeditions: Send heroes on expeditions to discover random parts

  • Events: Special events often reward high-quality parts
  • Merging: Combine 3 parts of the same quality to create 1 higher quality part

Refitting Strategy Tips

  • Energy Management: You cannot equip all Q7 parts (1740 energy needed vs 1080 capacity) - prioritize quality for your APC's main role
  • Focus on Main APC: Max out one vehicle's refitting before spreading resources
  • Balance Stats: Don't neglect defense - survivability matters in prolonged battles
  • Quality Over Quantity: One Q7 part is worth more than multiple Q5 parts
  • Match APC Role: Offensive APCs benefit most from Tier 2 weapons, defensive from Tier 3 armor
  • Save Diamonds: Only buy parts when on sale or for critical upgrades
  • Expedition Priority: Run expeditions daily - free parts add up over time

Understanding APC Systems

Levels & Supreme Levels

APCs have a two-tier leveling system:

  • Base Level: Primary progression that unlocks skills and increases stats
  • Supreme Level: Additional progression layer (max 50 for most APCs)
  • Both levels require specific upgrade materials
  • Higher quality APCs have higher level caps

Refitting System

Most APCs (except Wasteland APC) support refitting with upgrade parts:

  • 5 Tiers of refitting slots (Tier 1 through Tier 5)
  • Each tier can accept different part types
  • Ultimate Gadgets - Special parts that add powerful abilities
  • Parts provide stat bonuses and unlock active skills
  • Higher-tier parts require more resources but give better bonuses

Skills

APCs can have two types of skills:

  • Primary Skills: Active abilities used in combat
  • Secondary Skills: Passive bonuses always in effect
  • Skills level up as APC levels increase
  • Some skills are unlocked through specific gadgets

Quality Tiers

APCs are categorized by quality, which determines their potential:

  • Common (Tier 3): Wasteland APC - tutorial only
  • Rare (Tier 4): Plunderer - early game progression
  • Epic (Tier 5): Armed Chariot, Iron Fist, New World Protector, Hero Rally
  • Legendary (Tier 6): Demon, Neon, Pink Note - endgame power
